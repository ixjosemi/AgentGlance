# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, considerate, and specific.
- Assume good intent while taking responsibility for impact.
- Welcome questions and differing technical perspectives.
- Respect privacy and never publish another person's credentials or local session data.
- Accept maintainer decisions and correction gracefully.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner's GitHub profile or, when a security concern is involved, the private vulnerability-reporting flow described in `SECURITY.md`. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when representing the project elsewhere.
